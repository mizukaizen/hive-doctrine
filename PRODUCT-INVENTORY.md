## Pricing Philosophy

**v1 priced on document length. v2 prices on concept value.**

The question is not "how many words is this" but "how many hours/dollars does this save the buyer?" A 500-word framework that prevents a catastrophic agent permission leak is worth more than a 10,000-word research report. Tiers still exist, but individual prices within tiers now reflect the weight of the idea, not the weight of the file.

## 5. Content Guidelines

### Anonymisation Rules
- Author: **Melisia Archimedes** — never use real name
- Company: **The Hive Doctrine** — never reference melis.ai, OpenClaw by company name, or personal infrastructure
- Agents: Use generic names (e.g., "Agent Alpha" not "Marcus") unless the persona IS the product (Elliott is a public persona)
- Infrastructure: Genericise all IPs, domains, server names. "Production VPS" not "Helsinki"
- Wallet addresses, API keys, tokens: **NEVER include**. Not even redacted.
- X/Twitter/Reddit scraped content: **Must be rewritten**, not quoted. Ideas only, not verbatim.

### Hive Doctrine Framing
Every product should feel like it comes from a practitioner who has deployed these systems in production, not a theorist. The voice is:
- **Direct** — no hedging, no "it depends"
- **Opinionated** — clear recommendations, not options menus
- **Battle-tested** — reference real failure modes and fixes
- **Compressed** — say it in 500 words, not 5,000

### Metadata Header (every product file)
```yaml
---
title: [Product Name]
collection: [C1–C14 code]

```

### Tier Packaging Standards
- **Pollen** ($0): 500–1,500 words. Blog post or PDF. Email gate. Purpose: build list.
- **Doctrine** ($4.99–$9.99): 1,500–4,000 words. Single-concept deep dive. Gumroad PDF.
- **Honey** ($29–$149): 4,000–10,000 words OR template pack OR working config. The "this saves me 10+ hours" tier.
- **Nectar** ($49–$199): Multi-part system. Course module, video + PDF, or comprehensive framework with implementation guide.
- **Royal Jelly** ($299–$799): Complete business system. White-label potential. Includes templates, configs, case studies, and implementation guide.
