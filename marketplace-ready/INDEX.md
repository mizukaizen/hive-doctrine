### 2. Agentic Alpha
**File:** `honey/revenue/agentic-alpha.md`
**Price:** $49
**Length:** 3,000 words
**Audience:** Agent operators and service builders

**Summary:** Market map of profitable AI agent opportunities (2026). Ranks five money paths: 1) AI SDR agency ($10K–$50K/mo), 2) Customer support automation ($10K–$25K/build), 3) Vertical-specific agents ($5K–$20K/mo), 4) Infrastructure tools (VC-scale), 5) Token speculation (high risk). ### 3. Linear vs Autonomous
**File:** `honey/revenue/linear-workflow-philosophy.md`
**Price:** $29
**Length:** 3,000 words
**Audience:** Founders choosing between agent vs workflow architectures

**Summary:** Why deterministic AI workflows beat autonomous agents for business products. Backed by benchmark data: best agents achieve 10% task completion (humans: 78%). Covers seven high-leverage product opportunities (AI SDR, Legal AI, Research Assistant, etc.), unit economics, build templates, and positioning strategy. Linear workflows retain 85%+ customers; autonomous agents churn at 50–80%.

**Key insight:** Autonomous agents are sexy. Linear workflows are profitable.

### 5. The Automated SaaS Factory
**File:** `doctrine/automated-saas-pipeline.md`
**Price:** $4.99
**Length:** 2,500 words
**Audience:** Developers and SaaS founders

**Summary:** How close are we to zero-touch SaaS deployment? Answer: 80% automatable, 20% deliberately human-gated.
